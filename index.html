Welcome to the Warzone!

Below you will find the diffrent game modes in Warzone. For more information click the photo!

Caldera

Caldera changes that up and makes playing Warzone more dynamic. Unlike the older maps, Caldera has peaks and valleys, so gunfights are going to play out differently. Because players can get height on the enemy, it's now easier to scout nearby enemy players.

Rebirth

Rebuilt, redefined, reinforced — from its roots nearly a decade ago to Season Two Reloaded for Warzone and Call of Duty®: Vanguard Rebirth Island is the fast-paced Warzone experience developed by Beenox with the help of Raven Software.

Plunder

The main objective of Plunder is to collect $2 million before the enemy teams do. Up to 102 players in a game. Each team has 30 minutes to collect as much money as possible before the game ends. Money can be deposited in piggy banks found around the map.

Current Meta Guns

These are the current guns people are running with on Warzone

Caldera

Kilo

The Kilo 141 is favored for its incredible accuracy and damage potential at range. The weapon has been a staple of Warzone for a very long time and continues to be a fan-favorite weapon during Warzone Pacific Season 1. This weapon is suited for Caldera's long-range gunfights.

Plunder

Owen

The Owen Gun's naturally high mobility and quick rate of fire make it a beast in a close encounter – with the right attachments, it's a decent option in medium-range gunfights too. This weapon is currently on the rise in the current Warzone meta, and players are keen to find some overpowered builds.
